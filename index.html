Ghana Birthday

What we do ?

Ghana Birthday is a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and depending on their gender outputs their Akan name.

    Male names

  • Sunday - Kwasi
  • Monday - Kwadwo
  • Tuesday - Kwabena
  • Wednesday - Kwaku
  • Thursday - Yaw
  • Friday - Kofi
  • Saturday - Kwame

    Female names

  • Sunday - Akosua
  • Monday - Adwoa
  • Tuesday - Abenaa
  • Wednesday - Akua
  • Thursday - Yaa
  • Friday - Afua
  • Saturday - Ama
